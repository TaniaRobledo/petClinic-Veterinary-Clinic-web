Veterinary assistant and laboratory technician from the San Fernando Training Institute. His ongoing training leads him to study several courses on fluid therapy, transfusions, anesthesia, exotic animals, and rehabilitation.

Responsible for the clinic's laboratory, assisting in consultations and emergencies. A key member of our team. Attentive and always ready to facilitate the stay of clients and our patients during their visits to the clinic.
